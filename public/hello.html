How My Super Alter-Ego Came About...
A child of Miami, born and raised, design and science have always been two things I've juggled. Being a graduate of Design and Architecture Senior High School and Florida International University (BS in Biological Science), the two disciplines have almost refused to leave me. More recently, curiosity in web development has fused both my analytical and creative sides. HTML & CSS are my “sun” and Ruby is my “moon.” Some days they can even be referred to as my “comedy” and “drama.” At any rate, we all walk hand-in-hand in creation. In the midst of this creation, I transform. I am light and I am dark. I am rough and I am smooth. I am hot and I am cold. I am the past and I am the present. I become an alter ego of opposing super forces.
